Before You Send

A few things
worth knowing.

The answers to the questions we hear most often.

How far in advance should I book?

For flagship events, six to twelve months is ideal. Shorter timelines are considered case-by-case — send the inquiry and we'll tell you honestly if we can do it justice.

Does Gift travel internationally?

Yes. Travel, accommodation, and a small per-diem are typically arranged by the host for events outside Lagos. These details are finalized on the discovery call.

What does the fee cover?

Preparation (scripting, rehearsal, stakeholder calls), day-of presence, and a debrief after. Any travel, specialty styling, or additional content is quoted separately and transparently.

Can I book Gift for a private or family event?

She does a small number of private weddings, dedications, and milestone ceremonies each year. Please share the context in the form and we'll let you know quickly if it's a fit.

What's the booking policy?

A signed agreement and a 50% deposit secure the date. The balance is due 14 days before the event. Cancellation terms are detailed in the agreement.
